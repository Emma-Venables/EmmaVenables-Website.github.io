About The Artist



Emma Venables is a half British, half Maltese artist. She is currently reading for a Bachelors Degree in Fine Arts and Digital Arts at the University of Malta and has also spent some time studying at the Free University of Bozen-Bolzano, Italy.

Although having worked with traditional art, photography, and sculpture, Venables' preferred artistic method is digital illustration. Her work explores the surreal manifestation of common day objects and people reconfigured through intermittent designs.
